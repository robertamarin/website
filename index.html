Solutions & Services

Program-level ownership across inventory, parts, refurbishment, and movement.

Inventory & Storage Management

Problem → approach → outcomes

  • Problem: Stockouts, overbuys, and firefighting across SKUs and sites.
  • Approach: Slotting design, cycle counting cadence, min/max governance, vendor-managed inventory, critical spares programs.
  • Outcomes: 20-40% expedite reduction, cycle-count accuracy >99.5%, spares coverage with clear ownership.
Complex Parts & Equipment Programs

Problem → approach → outcomes

  • Problem: Engineering-grade kits and builds stall because suppliers, BOMs, and schedules don’t align.
  • Approach: Vendor coordination, BOM validation, build-to-order, QC, and delivery at point of use.
  • Outcomes: Lead-time reduction 15-30%, kit accuracy >99%, fewer line-down incidents.
Refurbishment & Lifecycle Programs

Problem → approach → outcomes

  • Problem: Aging assets and parts queues tie up capital and risk downtime.
  • Approach: Case-by-case rebuilds, OEM partnerships, dedicated teams for complex initiatives, streamlined catalogs for routine repairs.
  • Outcomes: Turnaround time predictability, asset life extension, controlled cost per rebuild.
Shipping & High-Visibility Logistics

Problem → approach → outcomes

  • Problem: High-value inventory moves with low visibility and poor exception handling.
  • Approach: Multi-site, end-to-end coverage; proactive exception alerts; accurate ETAs; POD and chain-of-custody proof.
  • Outcomes: Traceability for every move, fewer expedites, confident field and production schedules.
